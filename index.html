About Us

LIGHT SAMVEDNA

LiGHT Samvedna is a group of students from the Government College of Engineering Aurangabad working under the Student-run NGO of IIT Kharagpur called Gopali Youth Welfare Society. Light Samvedna is a center for people who think beyond themselves, who look beyond themselves, and care to support not just the less privileged but all the possible people who need support.

Activity

Blood Donation

The Samvedna Club organized the Blood Donation Camp in association with Government Medical College and Hospital Aurangabad. The Camp was inaugurated by Dr. P B Murnal, Dr. A M Nikalje. There was a huge response from the students; about 98 students donated their blood. The competition like poster Making, Rangoli also held on the day of blood donation.

Orphanage Visit

Our club regularly visits an orphanage located close to our college to help the children there for education. There are multiple objectives of the visit. These visits help those students with their studies. Along with that students are helped with any other difficulties they might be facing.

Cleanliness Drive

The best place to start cleaning is home. Samvedna students too believed in it and therefore chose their college to start cleaning. Respective Department were cleaned, unusable stuff were removed and cleanliness of basketball and Volleyball court was carried out.
